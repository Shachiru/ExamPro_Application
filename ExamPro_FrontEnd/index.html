Process

How ExamPro Works

Our streamlined process makes setting up and taking exams simple for everyone involved.

1

Create & Configure

Teachers create exams, add questions, set time limits, and configure security settings through an intuitive interface.

2

Notify & Schedule

ExamPro sends automated notifications to students about upcoming exams, complete with all necessary details and instructions.

3

Take & Submit

Students take exams in a secure environment with real-time countdown timers, and submit answers within the allotted time.

4

Auto-Grade & Review

Objective questions are automatically graded, while teachers can review and score subjective answers through the platform.

5

Analyze Results

Teachers can view detailed analytics and gain insights into student performance, identifying areas for improvement.

6

Share & Report

Results are securely shared with students through email notifications, with comprehensive reports available for download.
